Ruka Designs

At Ruka Designs, we specialize in custom furniture and wooden art made from reclaimed African hardwood. Having once sailed the Indian Ocean as traditional dhows, each slab of wood is handcrafted into a one-of-a-kind piece of art.

We believe in building bridges

Our logo symbolizes the traditional dhow, a timeless sailing vessel from the ancient Middle East. As traders ventured across the Indian Ocean to the East African shores, they brought the dhow with them where it has remained an integral part of Swahili culture almost unchanged over the millennia. Our hope is that as the dhow brought together distant cultures of antiquity, it will do the same in it's second life serving as a connection between each of you and the exotic shores of East Africa.

We believe in reusing and recycling

Our materials are sourced sustainably and directly from Kenya and Tanzania. We use a blend of African hardwoods including walnut, mahogany, teak, mango, and mangrove — all 100% reclaimed and ‘up-cycled’. By using reclaimed materials, we are keeping trees in the ground. Every tree left to grow to maturity can absorb more than 1 tonne of carbon dioxide in it's life and provide habitat for countless species.

We believe in supporting local communities

We value our relationships with the coastal communities and craftsmen we work with. Each purchase you make helps these communities monetize fishing vessels that would otherwise be discarded and provides jobs to craftsmen who pre-process all of our materials on site in Arusha, Tanzania.
